About:

Yanely's Photography started in 2014, this week actually. I have taken up photography and have started to indulge in all the aesthetics of it to try and become a better one and possibly a professional.

The camera I use is the iPhone 5c. For me it works great and always captures the image I like. When using the iPhone at the right angle and with the right lighting, everything is possible.

YP has been influenced by several different types of photography. Some of the photography that I like are (in order from best to least best):

  1. Nature
  2. Action
  3. Portraits
  4. Landscape
